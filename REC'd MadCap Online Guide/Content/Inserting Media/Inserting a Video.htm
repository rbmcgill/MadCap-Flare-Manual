Inserting a Video into a Topic

Inserting Multimedia is a very similar process except that you can also pull material from the internet.

 

  1. Open a new or existing topic in the XML Editor.
  2. Click the Insert tab on the Ribbon. The Multimedia section is the first from the left.
  3. Select a space in the document where you wish to place your video.
  4. Click the Multimedia button in the Multimedia section. The Insert Multimedia window appears.
  5. Find and select your video. Or select Multimedia from Web and type in the URL.
  6. Click the Open button. The video appears within your topic.
