Inserting Images

 

 

  1. Open a new or existing topic in the XML Editor.

  2. Click the Insert tab on the Ribbon. The Multimedia section is the first from the left.
  3. Select a space in the topic where you wish to place your image.
  4. Click the Image button in the Multimedia section or within the XML Editor window. The Insert Image window appears.
  5. Click the elipses button (image). The Open File dialog box appears.
  6. Find and select your image.
  7. Click the Open button.
  8. Add a Screen Tip and Alternate Text here for accessibility.
  9. Click the OK button when you are ready to insert the image. The Copy to Project window appears.
  10. Click the OK button in this window to confirm you wish to place the image. The image appears within your topic.
